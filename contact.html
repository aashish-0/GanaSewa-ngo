Gana Sewa is a Society formed & registered in the year 2008 in Guwahati, Assam with the objective of working on improving the social condition of people. We do this through well-planned and comprehensive programmes in health, education, livelihoods. Our overall goal is the empowerment of women and girls from poor and marginalised communities, leading to improvement in their lives and livelihoods. We believe that if you made a man learn how to cook (Skill) you are making him self reliant & thus he can enjoy reputable status in society.
